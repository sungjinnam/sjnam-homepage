Projects

  • From 2017, I am also working on a project called Analytics for LEarners As People (LEAP) . The goal of this project is to build a new generation of learning analytics tools which integrate our understanding of “student as a person” and “student as a learner” across various kinds of data, including traditional learning analytics data and other behavioral signals. Specifically, I am working on representing student's knowledge state from discussion forum dialogue texts, and predicting the amount of information exist in a sentence for describing a particular concept.
  • From 2014, I have worked on a intelligent tutoring system: Dynamic Support of Contextual Vocabulary Acquisition for Reading (DSCoVAR) . We aim to help kids in middle school improve their literacy skills and develop some compelling new technology in the process. In this project, I am working on modeling student's engagement and developing a scale for partial knowledge state in the vocabulary acquisition task.
